The Coalition of Gnomes

Guild Charter

I have assembled this guild of Gnomes to honor the following tasks. Each member shall honorably undertake, to the best of their ability, the mental and physical fortitude required to ratify a Gnome kingdom deserving of our name. For we are The Coalition of Gnomes. Henceforth known as The COG.

  1. We will retake Gnomeregan!
  2. In doing so, we shall bring Mekgineer Thermaplugg to justice!
  3. Demand equal rights for Gnomes as a part of the Alliance.
  4. Form our own kingdom with the aid of our allies:
    • Knights of Gnomeregan
    • Clan Hammersong

Instituted and sworn by,
Gideon Killerwatt
